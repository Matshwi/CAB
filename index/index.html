message from the admin

My name is Kgothatso Motsogi a final year student in Bachelors of Law at the University of South Africa. I have been with Dikeletsong Citizens Advice Bureau (DCAB) for almost 3years.

I remember quite vividly when we joined DCAB we came from an Organization called Dynamic Law Student Organization, which ultimately served the purpose of breaching the generic gap between theoretical and practical legal education with the aim to breed competence for our law students.

At some point we were raising funds to attend a moot court event that was closely aligned to our practical legal training education. Two volunteers from DCAB attended our fund raising event and gave us one of the most amazing opportunities that continues to pivot a significant role in my career, by recruiting us and rather giving us an opportunity to volunteer for the organization.

When I first came as volunteer I could not draft or rather comprehend letters, conduct telephone calls and most importantly consult with the public which are our primary stakeholders that we continue to serve, but however with the help and support from the more experienced volunteers I was given an opportunity to acquire the skills and knowledge that till this day sustains my abilities in the line of work I do.

DCAB not only helps the vulnerable and the public who have nowhere to go, but it also serves prominence in our student journey predominantly in our practical side of our legal education. So I will forever be indebted and humbled by the opportunity that DCAB contributed in our journey.

Kgothatso Motsogi
